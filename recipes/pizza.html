Pizza

Description:

A popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
