Lasagna

Description:

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef with onions and garlic.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce.
  7. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
