Chicken Korma

Description:

A rich and creamy Indian curry made with tender chicken in a spiced yogurt-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook for another minute.
  5. Add coriander powder, garam masala, and mix well.
  6. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
  7. Pour in cream and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
